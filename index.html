AR Glasses

AR(augmented reality) is defined as a system that incorporates 3 basic features : Combination of real and virtual world, Real time interaction and Accurate 3D registration of virtual and real objects.Using these glasses we ensure that we can operate smartphones just by reading hand gesture in air. To achieve it we have attached camera at its 2 ends and we use computer vision,ML to recognise hand gesture . This gesture will be send to the smartphones via bluetooth thereby making our smart phones respond to those instruction.

Marjanator

Marjanator is water traversing bot that is currently semi-autonomous. It will rover on top of the water and collect waste out of it. It's based on a simple model having a wide opening to collect waste fitted with a strainer at the back. It will use a camera to scan the surface of the water body, and then specify the direction over which the trash is drifting. Using paddle wheels, it will slowly hover over the surface and will have all the waste collected inside the empty container. Using this way removes most of the macro-plastic and wastes that are harmful to the aquatic biosphere without use of any chemicals.

Exoskeleton

The idea behind this project is to develop an upper body exoskeleton for assisting over-head tasks, which is simple, light in weight, and cheap so that everyone can afford it as compared to presently commercially available exoskeletons. It is a passive exoskeleton as it does not use any active power source. The design is quite simple. Shoulder joint of the exoskeleton has 3 degrees of freedom to allow user to move their hands around freely, which is comprised of three rotary joints.

For amplification of torque, the suit uses two torsion springs and two telescope conical springs. The torsion springs are connected at the back of the suit, whereas the telescopic springs are at the front. The springs help the wearer to keep his arms aver head for longer period of time which reduces fatigue.

Torminator

Torminator, the robotic arm, is a high profession farming bot which can be controlled with the help of a remote controller. The Robotic arm is made of Aluminium and is meant for lifting loads, removing obstacles and collecting samples. It has 6 degrees of freedom (6-DOF). It consists of 2 links, of aluminium, and has an end effector for grasping objects which are connected by revolute joints. The end effector is a three-fingered gripper. Soft foam rubber is placed on each finger to grip soft material without causing damage to the surface due to the excessive force from the fingers of the gripper.

It has 7 servo motors and 1 DC motor for the rotation of the arm on the base. Inverse kinematics is used to control the position of the end effector, allowing for better control of the arm. 4 LiPo Battery is used to power the arm and Jetson Nano and Arduino are used for the controlling of the arm.

Talking Bot

Taking bot is a machine learning based bot designed to have simple conversions with humans. It uses Transfer Learning to build a State-of-the-Art dialog agent based on OpenAI's GPT-2 Transformer language model.

The idea behind this approach is quite simple:
start by pretraining a language model on a very large corpus of text to be able to generate long stretches of contiguous coherent text, fine-tune this language model to adapt it to our end-task: dialog.

The model is given a prior consisting of part of a conversation. Then the network generates one paragraph to complete one answer in the conversation. Next we can add our own response and the cycle repeats.

The ability to recognise and output speech is also added using open-source models and libraries.



Wall Plotter

This bot takes input and draws by translating itself using polar coordinates.It can draws various svg images and has a graphical interphase for customizing images.The way it works is taking image file in the form of SVG file and then extracting coordinates of the paths which are to be drawn. Using these information the string of the plotter changes its length to get desired shapes and designs. It changes its length also for the rotation in the stepper motor,the clockwise rotation will increase length and anticlockwise will decrese.

FIDO

It is intelligent featured and remotely operated sperical orb that moves and rotates freely.It is easy to control and has a robust design. As per its shape it has translation and rotational motion. It has a central horizontal axle which is fixed with two bearings that is screwed to the two shell attached caps and has 2 DC motors attached to it A vertical axle is attached to midpoint of horizontal axle, whose length is less than the sphere’s radius. At the lowerend of the vertical axle,we have attached two heavy steel disks where all the batteries and sensors will be clamped.

Pipe Traversing Bot

This bot automate the periodic inspection and maintenance of gas pipelines. This bot travells through these pipes, inspects and send the results periodically. These can travel to the pipes where it is difficult for a human reach and even it can repair the defect which in turn save money, time and labour.
